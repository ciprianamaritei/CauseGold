Why CauseGold?

CauseGold offers a secure and trusted place to sell or donate your gold and silver. Our philosophy is to offer our clients openness and among the highest payouts in the industry while helping organizations fundraise for their Causes.

  • Donate your payouts to the Cause of your choice.
  • Sell you gold or silver and support Causes just by using CauseGold.
  • Buy from the CauseGold store to Donate to the Cause of your choice.
